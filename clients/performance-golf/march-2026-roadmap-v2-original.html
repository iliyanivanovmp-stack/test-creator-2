March 2026 Testing Roadmap

Performance Golf is moving from Checkout Champ to Shopify. The old system's profitability depended on post-purchase video upsells, a mechanism that doesn't exist on the new platform yet. Rebuilding it is the top priority: without it, the economics don't work.

The second priority is trust. Across 35+ recent Trustpilot reviews, customers cite unwanted subscription charges and hidden fees. Yet PG holds the strongest guarantee in the competitive set (365 days vs. the industry average of 60) and has 800K+ clubs sold, 1M+ subscribers, and 3,700+ reviews at 4.8 stars. These signals are invisible at the point of purchase.

Slots 1 & 2 Custom App

Post-Purchase Video Upsell App

Post-purchase page (order confirmation flow)
Impact to be measured
Rebuild the post-purchase video upsell flow from the old Checkout Champ platform as a custom Shopify app. Customers see a video after purchase, and at timed intervals, a one-click product offer appears. This is the profitability layer the new store needs to match the economics of the old setup.
Slot 3 A/B Test

Cart Drawer Trust + Savings

Cart drawer (site-wide)
Impact to be measured
Add trust signals, savings visibility, and an optional product upsell to the cart drawer. The current drawer has zero reassurance at the moment of purchase, despite PG holding a 365-day guarantee that is 6x the competitive average. Testing trust-first (V1) vs. trust + upsell (V2) to isolate what drives the lift.
