Insights

The site's strongest assets (409 clinicians on FrontrowMD, 6,328+ reviews, 60-day guarantee, a unique Arctic botanical story) are buried under countdown timers, conflicting discounts, and urgency tactics that customers call "scammy." The ads bring them in. The pages push them away.

Your top 3 Meta ads have run 5+ months. They convert. But the landing pages don't continue their story. Ad #1 targets parents of teens with 25% off, landing page shows 45% off. Ad #3 tells an ingredient education story, landing page replaces it with a discount headline. The ads do their job. The pages aren't finishing it.

01
A/B Test /pages/stop-acne, 258,929 sessions/mo

Landing Page Trust & Message Match

Replace the discount-first hero on your highest-traffic landing page with trust-led messaging that matches what your ads promise. We'll test whether leading with credibility (clinician endorsement, reviews, guarantee) or ingredient education (Arctic botanicals story) converts better than urgency tactics.

Your best-selling product is actually 3 items in one ritual, but the page presents it as a single product at a single price. We'll test whether breaking down the bundle value or elevating trust signals above the price does more to overcome the price objection.

Month 2 Focus
PDP Optimization + Agentic Commerce
Shopify is launching ChatGPT as an agentic storefront channel. Buyers will discover and purchase products directly inside ChatGPT. What the AI surfaces depends on product content: gallery images, descriptions, and pricing structure. Three PDP tests to make sure Norse shows up right.
This Month
01
A/B Test Best-seller PDPs

Product Gallery Optimization

Test an optimized gallery sequence that leads with texture close-ups of the balm and beeswax products, application shots, and before/after results instead of the current tin-on-white photography. AI shopping agents pull gallery images as primary product signals.
* Concept illustration only. Final design will differ.
02
A/B Test Kill Acne & Redness Ritual PDP

Product Description Restructure

Rewrite the product description to lead with ingredients and the Arctic botanical story instead of discount claims. AI agents parse descriptions to match buyer intent. A description that says "tamanu oil for bacterial acne" will surface for "natural acne treatment" queries. One that says "SAVE 45%" won't.
* Concept illustration only. Final design will differ.

A/B Test Kill Acne & Redness Ritual PDP
