Executive Summary

98.34% of sessions end without an add-to-cart: the biggest leak in the funnel. Product galleries with 20-30+ images create an unusable thumbnail wall with no sequential browsing, and the homepage leads with discounts despite service being the #1 purchase driver (4.3/5 vs. price at 4.0/5). Month 1 targets both pages with layout and messaging restructures. Month 2 replaces the notification-based add-to-cart flow with a cart drawer to reduce friction between browsing and checkout.
