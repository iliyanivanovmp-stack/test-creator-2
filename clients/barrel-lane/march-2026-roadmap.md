# March 2026 Testing Roadmap

**Data Sources:** Competitor Research, Customer Voice Research, Site Audit

---

## The Problem

Barrel Lane's product pages lack social proof and tasting guidance. These are the two biggest conversion drivers in premium spirits DTC. Every major Australian competitor (Starward, Archie Rose, Four Pillars) leads with reviews, awards, and detailed tasting notes. Barrel Lane doesn't.

Gift buyers (a high-intent segment for $120+ spirits) have no dedicated pathway. No bundles, no gift wrapping, no "perfect for" messaging.

**The opportunity:** Close the trust gap on product pages and capture gift buyers.

---

## Test #1: PDP Social Proof & Tasting Notes

**Page:** PDP

**Hypothesis:** If we add customer reviews and detailed tasting notes to product pages, ATC rate will increase because premium spirit buyers need trust signals and flavour guidance to justify $120+ purchases, and every major AU competitor already provides this.

**Data:** Competitor audit: all 5 competitors display reviews + tasting profiles on PDP. Customer voice research: "Is it worth $120?" is the #1 objection; tasting guidance and social proof are the top purchase drivers in craft spirits.

**Variation:** Add to each PDP: star rating + review count below product title, 3-5 customer reviews above the fold, visual tasting notes section (flavour profile, nose/palate/finish, food pairings), and an "Australian Made, Small Batch" trust badge near ATC.

**Brief:** Design: review display component, tasting notes visual (flavour wheel or simple list), trust badge. Dev: integrate Shopify review app (e.g. Judge.me), add tasting notes metafield to product template, badge placement near ATC. Can use existing review app or embed manually.

---

## Test #2: Homepage Gift Buyer Experience

**Page:** Homepage

**Hypothesis:** If we add a visible gift pathway on the homepage, conversion from gift-intent visitors will increase because spirits are a major gifting category and there's currently no dedicated entry point for gift buyers.

**Data:** Customer voice: gift buyers are the second-largest segment for premium spirits. Competitor audit: Archie Rose offers "Build your own gift box," Starward has "Whisky flight" bundles, Four Pillars runs seasonal gift sets. Barrel Lane has none.

**Variation:** Add a "Gifts" section to the homepage: hero-adjacent placement with a curated gift bundle (e.g. "The Barrel Lane Gift Set", whisky + tasting glass), headline "The Perfect Gift for Whisky Lovers," and a dedicated "Gifts" item in primary navigation linking to a gift collection page.

**Brief:** Design: gift section block for homepage (mobile-first), gift collection page layout. Dev: create gift bundle product or collection in Shopify, add homepage section, add nav item. Requires gift products to be created/bundled first.
