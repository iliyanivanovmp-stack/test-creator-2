## Customer Surveys (Post-Purchase, ~476 responses, Jan 19 - Feb 18, 2026)

**Question:** "Did anything almost stop you from purchasing today?"

**Top objection themes:**

| Theme | Approx. Frequency | Key Quotes |
|-------|-------------------|------------|
| Price/cost | ~50%+ | "The price is crazy honestly," "Very expensive even on sale," "If I hadn't received a gift card, I would not have made a purchase" |
| Size availability | ~10% | "Out of my size in most patterns I liked," "Sizes were limited" |
| Shipping costs | ~8% | "Shipping not free even with a $168 order," "Too high of a threshold for free shipping," "High shipping costs!" |
| Sizing uncertainty | ~7% | "Lack of garment measurements... armpit to armpit, widest part of hips," "No size chart," "Conflicting fit reviews," "Unclear on new sizing" |
| Website bugs | ~5% | "Checkout button failed to load on mobile and desktop," "Dead white screen on mobile, had to move to laptop," "Shopping bag kept dropping my order," "Website was crashing" |
| Final sale anxiety | ~4% | "First time buyer so sizing on no return sale item," "Worried about sizing and can't return," "The fact that I can not return the items" |
| Discount/coupon issues | ~3% | "$20 off did not work," "Didn't receive the discount code after signing up," "Rewards points not working" |

**Notable technical issues from surveys:**
- Checkout button failed to load on mobile and desktop (required refresh)
- Mobile showed sizes as sold out that were available on desktop
- Dead white screen on mobile during warehouse sale (had to switch to laptop)
- Size filter pop-up wouldn't scroll to tall sizes on PC
- Shopping bag/cart kept dropping orders
- Apple Pay not working
- Package protection showing "sold out"
- Website freezing/crashing during high-traffic periods

**Notable qualitative insights:**
- "Eco satin sounds sus" (fabric trust issue)
- "Your checkout process is way too long"
- "All these f@#*^$! questions and pop up ads at check out" (checkout friction)
- "I couldn't easily find the size guide, I had to google search for it"
- "Difficulty navigating... not the most helpful filters"
- "It would be nice to see filters like 'tops' or 'shorts'"
- "Different variations/colors of the same design should all be available when you click on the item"
- "No PayPal pay in 4"
- "If you didn't accept PayPal, I would have not purchased"
- Multiple mentions of discount codes/rewards points not working

**What customers love:**
- Print designs are the #1 purchase driver

## Competitor Research

| Feature | Printfresh | Lake Pajamas | Lunya | Desmond & Dempsey |
|---------|-----------|-------------|-------|-------------------|
| Free shipping | $195+ | $75+ | $150+ | Free US shipping |
| Sizing approach | XXS-6X, inconsistent across fabrics | True to size, accounts for shrinkage, inseam lengths listed | Size by bust measurement, free exchanges 30 days | Detailed size & fit page, drawstring pants |
| Fabric story | "Eco Satin" (searched 1,759 times, called "sus") | "Softest Pajamas Imaginable," Peruvian Pima Cotton, "cashmere of cotton" | "Washable Silk" (trademarked), bluesign certified, proprietary fabric names | Hand-painted prints, print storytelling tied to travel |
| Value framing | Sale-driven | Bundles | Membership program, 15% member discount | Free monogramming included |
| Navigation | Print discovery via search only | By fabric/style | By fabric type in nav (Washable Silk, Cottonsilk, etc.) | By print story |
| Gifting | Not prominent | Strong gifting (postpartum gift reputation) | Dedicated gift shop, gift wrapping | Free monogramming |
| Payment options | Standard + Klarna | Standard | Standard | Klarna, Afterpay, Square |

**Key gaps for Printfresh:**
1. Free shipping threshold is the highest in category ($195 vs. $75-$150 or free)
2. No fabric storytelling on site. "Eco Satin" lacks credibility vs. competitors' trademarked, certified fabric narratives.
3. Sizing info is weak. Customers ask for measurements. Lake provides inseam lengths. Lunya offers free exchanges.
4. Print-driven navigation is missing. 114K searches are heavily print-name driven, but there's no browse-by-print in the nav.

## Cross-Source Analysis: Converging Themes

**Theme A: Price/Value Perception (5+ sources)**
Survey (50%+ cite price), Reviews (love product after buying), Site Search ("sale" 1,553), Competitor Research (highest shipping threshold), Inspiration (Quince transparent pricing). Price is the #1 objection but reviews prove customers love the product. This is a value communication problem, not a pricing problem.

**Theme B: Sizing Anxiety (4+ sources)**
Survey (7% cite sizing, want measurements), Reviews (inconsistency across fabrics, "fit more like a Large than a Small"), Competitor Research (Lake inseam lengths, Lunya free exchanges, Faherty True Fit), Previous Tests (PF07 won on collection, PF10 lost on PDP). Amplified by final sale policy. PF24 and Size Guide & Quiz CTA already queued.

**Theme C: Ad-to-LP Message Match (3+ sources)**
Ads (2/3 drive to MacKenzie-Childs with generic grid LP), Shopify Analytics (57K sessions), Inspiration (LoveShackFancy collection storytelling). Clear gap between what ads promise and what landing pages deliver.

**Theme D: Navigation & Print Discovery (3+ sources)**
Site Search (114K searches dominated by print names), Survey ("difficulty navigating," "not helpful filters"), Shopify Analytics (81% mobile). Navigation forces users into search. Print-first browsing is missing.

**Theme E: Fabric Education (3+ sources)**
Survey ("Eco satin sounds sus"), Site Search ("eco satin" 1,759, "flannel" 782), Reviews (post-purchase fabric praise), Competitor Research (Lunya trademarked fabrics, Lake "cashmere of cotton"). Gap between pre-purchase skepticism and post-purchase love.

**Theme F: Cart-to-Checkout Drop (3+ sources)**
Shopify Analytics (58% drop from ATC to checkout), Survey (shipping costs #3 objection), Previous Tests (cart drawer wins 3/3). Cart is proven optimization territory. PF16/PF22 testing shipping price/threshold, but cart communication is untested.

**Theme G: Mobile Experience (3+ sources)**
Shopify Analytics (81% of sessions), Survey (dead white screen, checkout failures, filter bugs, "difficult to order on phone"), Heatmaps (average fold at filter bar on mobile). No mobile-specific tests run yet.
