Executive Summary

We analyzed Shopify analytics, heatmaps, customer surveys, site search, reviews, Meta ads performance, competitor research, and your content calendar to identify where the biggest conversion gaps are. The concepts below target the pages and components where the data shows the clearest opportunities: the product page, the gifting experience ahead of Mother's Day, product discovery for new visitors, and cross-sell.

April Testing Concepts
01
Slot 1 A/B Test

PDP Cleanup + Product Information

Product Page

The product page is cluttered. On mobile, visitors scroll past gallery images, pricing, social proof, size selectors, trust icons, and gift wrapping before reaching any product description. Fabric details, care instructions, and the product story all sit inside collapsed accordions that heatmap data shows most visitors never open. PDP cleanup tests have won historically on this site.

Slot 2 A/B Test

Mother's Day Gifting Experience

Collection Page

The brand's top-performing ad sells "the gift they'll love unwrapping," but the landing page is an undifferentiated product grid with no gifting context. Reviews mention gifting nearly 100 times in 30 days. With Mother's Day on May 11, April is the critical ramp, and no dedicated gifting experience exists on site today.

Slot 3 A/B Test

Print Persona Quiz

Standalone Quiz Page

Over 41,000 site searches in 30 days are dominated by print names: cats, dachshunds, horses, pastries. More than half of visitors are new and have no efficient way to navigate a high-SKU catalog. The brand already maps prints to lifestyle personas in email marketing. A quiz translates that into a shoppable on-site experience that reduces decision paralysis.

PDP Product Upsells

Product Page

The product page has generic recommendation sections below the fold, but nothing curated or contextual to the product being viewed. Print-based browsing dominates site search, confirming customers want to explore within the same print family.

05
Slot 5 A/B Test

Sizing Guide Effectiveness

Product Page

Sizing uncertainty is the #3 purchase objection. Survey respondents say the current fit recommendation tool is unhelpful and they would rather see raw garment measurements. Combined with final sale policies and premium price points, every wrong-size order becomes a lost customer. This test isolates whether the size guide, the fit quiz, or both are actually building purchase confidence.

06
Slot 6 A/B Test

Homepage Second Fold

Homepage

The homepage drives nearly 88,000 sessions per month and heatmaps show strong engagement through the hero and category tiles. But engagement drops sharply at the testimonial section below. A press/social proof bar and a redesigned featured products section in this high-visibility zone could capture visitors before they bounce. Previous homepage module tests have won on this site.
