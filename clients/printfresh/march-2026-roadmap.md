# March 2026 Testing Roadmap

---

## Test #1: Ad to LP Message Match

**Page:** Landing Page

**Hypothesis:** If we align the landing page first fold with the top-performing ad creative, conversion rate will increase because the current experience breaks message match between what the ad promises and what the visitor sees on arrival.

**Variation:** 4 variations for the first fold, matching the messaging and visual style of the top ad to LP combo.

**Brief:** Design: 4 first-fold variations (mobile-first). Copy needs client approval before build.

**Deliverable:** 4 variations for the first fold

**Client requirement:** They need to approve the copy

## Test #4: Product Image Test - Top 3 Products (Agentic Commerce)
