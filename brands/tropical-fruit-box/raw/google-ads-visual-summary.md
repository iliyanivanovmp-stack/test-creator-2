**Offers and CTAs:** Free Shipping referenced in at least one visible ad. No explicit discount codes visible in the shopping ad headlines. CTAs are implicit (clicking the product card).

**Visual approach:** All Shopping ads show product photography — teal/turquoise branded gift boxes with fruit arrangements. Consistent brand color palette across all visible cards. Photography is bright, colorful, and product-focused. Some images show fruit outside the box for variety.

**Gaps vs. Meta:** Meta ads are running active discount codes (META15, SWEET15, 20% off $89+) while Google Shopping ads show no code-based offers in visible headlines. Google ads are product/SKU-level; Meta ads are brand and offer-level. No messaging overlap on gifting angle or "exotic fruits you didn't know existed" positioning visible in Google Shopping.
