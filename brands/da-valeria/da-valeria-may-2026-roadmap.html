Maggio 2026 CRO Testing Roadmap
Overview

Da Valeria has a verified 48-hour delivery reputation, confirmed across four years of Trustpilot reviews, but the site's above-the-fold experience doesn't reflect it. The homepage leads with a generic seasonal discount, product pages carry zero reviews despite strong Trustpilot ratings, and the cart shows a shipping fee with no nudge toward the €59 free shipping threshold. All three major conversion opportunities are inside the site, not in ad spend.

Slot 1 A/B Test
PDP Trust Overhaul: Reviews + Buy Box Cleanup
Page: Product Detail Pages

Trustpilot reviews from 2021 to 2025 confirm strong product satisfaction across the catalog, yet no reviews appear on any product detail page. Sidebar banners actively link visitors away from the product they are viewing, and a PayPal financing banner for purchases between €120 and €5,000 appears above the product title on a €5.20 item, creating irrelevant friction at the exact moment the purchase decision happens.

Slot 2 A/B Test
Homepage Hero: Delivery Speed vs. Generic Discount
Page: Homepage

Fast delivery is Da Valeria's most consistently cited competitive strength, mentioned in virtually every Trustpilot review since 2021, yet the homepage hero leads with a generic seasonal discount and no delivery promise above the fold. No direct competitor, including Kasanova, positions on delivery speed in their hero, leaving this UVP completely unowned in the market.

Slot 3 A/B Test
Cart: Free Shipping Progress Bar + AOV Nudge
Page: Cart Drawer

A shopper with €39 in their cart is €20 away from free shipping, but the cart shows only a €5.90 shipping line with no indicator of proximity to the €59 threshold. The cart UI also displays entirely in English on an Italian-language site, a localization bug that degrades trust at the most critical moment in the purchase funnel.

Biggest Killers of Conversion Rate
  • 1 Paid traffic lands on mismatched pages: the kitchen organizer ad sends visitors to a homepage showing outdoor products and a cherry blossom hero. The garden chair ad sends visitors to a raw search results page titled "RISULTATI RICERCA" with a sidebar featuring a blender, a hair dryer, and an inflatable mattress.
  • 2 A verified fast-delivery reputation is invisible on-site. "Velocissima," "ultrarapida," and "2 giorni lavorativi" appear in the majority of Trustpilot reviews across four years. The homepage buries this in the announcement bar. No competitor leads with it, making it a first-mover UVP that Da Valeria is not using.
  • 3 Product pages suppress conversion at the decision moment: zero reviews surfaced despite consistent Trustpilot ratings, sidebar banners link visitors off the page, and a financing widget for purchases between €120 and €5,000 appears on a €5.20 item.
  • 4 The cart recovers zero AOV from the €59 free shipping threshold. A customer with €39 in their cart has no way to know they are €20 away from free shipping. The cart UI is entirely in English on an Italian-language site, and there are no cross-sells or trust signals at checkout.
  • 5 Mobile LCP of 9.5 seconds against a 2.5-second threshold. The page takes nearly 10 seconds to visually complete on mobile. This is a conversion ceiling that limits the impact of every other improvement on the site.

Findings by Source
Meta Ads (3 ads analyzed, April-May 2026)
  • 2 of 3 analyzed ads send paid traffic to pages with no connection to the ad's product or category.
  • Ad 1 (kitchen organizer, €14.90) lands on a homepage hero showing outdoor furniture and a seasonal cherry blossom campaign.
  • Ad 3 (PP garden chair, €39.00) lands on a raw search results page with a sidebar showing a blender, inflatable mattress, and hair dryer. One product card shows a 948-day countdown timer, likely a technical glitch.
  • Ad 2 passes: the Tescoma apple slicer links directly to the correct product page.
Reviews & UGC (Trustpilot, Aug 2021 - Dec 2025)
  • Fast delivery is the top positive across four years: "Spedizione e ricezione velocissima," "Consegna ultrarapida," "Arrivato due giorni dopo l'acquisto."
  • Product quality consistently matches descriptions and images per reviewer feedback.
  • The first-purchase discount is confirmed as appreciated: "Ho usufruito di uno sconto del 10% per primo acquisto."
  • Main friction: GLS courier reliability in some provinces, no floor delivery for heavy furniture, assembly instructions not always included in packaging.
Page Speed / Core Web Vitals (Mobile, May 2026)
  • LCP of 9.5 seconds on mobile fails the Core Web Vitals threshold of 2.5 seconds. Performance score: 68/100.
  • Render-blocking requests account for an estimated 2,802ms of delay. Unoptimized images add approximately 566 KB of unnecessary weight.
  • CLS passes at 0.05. INP at 118ms needs improvement. Unused JavaScript accounts for an estimated 213 KB.
Competitor Research (May 2026)
  • Kasanova and Da Valeria share identical €59 free shipping thresholds and 10% first-order discount mechanics.
  • Kasanova runs a VIP loyalty card with member-exclusive pricing. Da Valeria has no equivalent program.
  • Duebi Casalinghi displays its 4.8/5 rating (611 reviews) prominently on the homepage. Da Valeria surfaces no reviews anywhere on-site.
  • Neither Kasanova nor Duebi Casalinghi leads with delivery speed as a primary UVP. The position is unclaimed in the market.
Site Screenshots (May 2026)
  • The cart drawer displays entirely in English ("CART," "Subtotal," "Shipping," "BUY") on an Italian-language site.
  • A €5.20 product page shows a PayPal BNPL banner for purchases between €120 and €5,000, positioned above the product title.
  • Two sidebar banners on product pages link visitors away from the product they are currently viewing.
  • The free shipping threshold (€59) and PRIMO10 discount code appear only in the announcement bar. Neither appears in the hero, on product pages, or in the cart.
