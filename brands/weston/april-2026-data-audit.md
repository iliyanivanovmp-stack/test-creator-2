## Cross-Source Analysis: Converging Themes

### 1. Trust Signal Inconsistency (Critical)

The warranty claim changes on every page of the site:

- Announcement bar: "3-Year Warranty"
- Homepage trust bar: "3 year Warranty"
- Glove Liners PDP trust badges: "2-Year Warranty"
- Collection page trust bar: "1-Year Warranty"
- Cart drawer trust badges: "3-year warranty"

Similarly, "Free shipping" is claimed in the collection page trust bar, but the cart drawer shows a ~$200 threshold. Review count fluctuates between "Over 6,000 reviews" (mobile homepage) and "Over 8,000 reviews" (desktop homepage, collection page). These inconsistencies undermine credibility, especially for a brand asking $80-$190 per product.

### 2. Raynaud's Disease: Untapped High-Intent Segment (Reviews x Site)

At least 6 glove liner reviewers and 2 sock reviewers explicitly mention Raynaud's/Reynaud's syndrome as their reason for purchasing. This is a medical condition affecting an estimated 5-10% of the population that drives strong purchase intent for heated products. Yet:

- No mention of Raynaud's on the homepage
- No mention in PDP hero bullets (small mention of "cold hands" and "circulation" exists)
- No dedicated landing page
- No ad targeting this audience
- Competitors haven't strongly claimed this positioning either, creating a first-mover opportunity

### 3. Message Match Failure (Ads x Site)

The top 3 best-performing ads reveal a systemic problem:

- Glove-specific ad copy lands on a generic homepage (2 of 3 ads)
- Black Friday ad with a 20% discount code lands on a page showing 50% off
- Only 1 of 3 ads has proper ad-to-page alignment (Ad #2 → Glove PDP)
- No dedicated landing pages exist for any campaign

### 4. Perpetual Sale Undermines Urgency (Ads x Site)

Multiple signals suggest the sale never ends:

- "ENDS: TODAY" on homepage
- "Last Day Holiday Sale" in announcement bar
- "50% OFF" on homepage
- "Our Black Friday Sale is NOW LIVE!" in ad running since January
- Products show 21-73% discounts simultaneously
This erodes trust with repeat visitors and contradicts the urgency messaging.

### 5. Homepage Undersells the Product Story (Reviews x Ads x Site)

Reviews reveal powerful, specific reasons people buy: Raynaud's relief, Kilimanjaro expeditions, hunting in -20°F weather, European Christmas markets, horseback riding. The homepage shows none of this. It leads with "50% OFF" and generic brand copy. Press logos (Good Housekeeping, People) are buried below the fold. Only 1 review is visible despite 8,000+ total.

## Client-Actionable Items (Not A/B Tests)

These issues should be fixed directly:

1. **Fix warranty inconsistency.** Pick one warranty term and apply it consistently site-wide (announcement bar, trust bars, PDP badges, cart drawer).
2. **Fix review count inconsistency.** Standardize whether it's 6,000 or 8,000+ across all pages.
3. **Remove or update the Black Friday ad (Ad #3).** It's March. Running a "Black Friday Sale is NOW LIVE!" ad 4 months later hurts credibility.
4. **Update "Rated Best Heated Socks of 2023"** on the socks PDP. It's 2026.
5. **Fix sticky ATC bar bug** on socks PDP: shows "2 Packs" but displays the 1-pack price ($79.95).
6. **Clarify free shipping policy.** Either free shipping is included (as the collection page trust bar claims) or there's a ~$200 threshold (as the cart drawer shows). Not both.
7. **Investigate battery auto-add behavior** that reviewer Linda D. flagged as deceptive.
8. **Clean up collection page categories.** Remove irrelevant filter options like "Motor Vehicle Climate Control" and "Mugs" from the main collection.
9. **Resolve navigation overlap.** Heated Jackets and Heated Vests appear under Men, Women, AND Apparel. Simplify paths.
10. **Add more product images** to the socks PDP (currently only 1 image vs. multiple on the glove liners PDP).
