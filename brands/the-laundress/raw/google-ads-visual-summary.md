# Google Ads Visual Summary

**Screenshots reviewed:** google-ads-1.png, google-ads-2.png
**Ads visible:** Mix of search text ads, shopping/product listing ads, display image ads, and video ad thumbnails. All attributed to "The Laundress LLC / Verified."

**Headline themes:**
- Brand/category: "The Laundress® Home & Laundry - Luxury Laundry & Home Care"
- Educational/how-to: "How To Clean Cashmere," "How To Wash Wool, Down, Fiberfill & Synthetic Blend Coats," "How To Care for Swimwear | Tips for Washing Swimsuits," "8 Reasons To Use Wool Dryer Balls"
- Product-specific: "Luxury Fabric Care Products - Fights Fresh & Set-In Stains," "Barry's Activewear Collection"
- Shopping titles: "Signature Detergent Classi…," "Darks Detergent," "Stain Solution," "Activewear Detergent," "Whites Detergent," "Beauty Sleep Laundry…," "Indulgent Detergent Collection"

**Offers and CTAs:**
- "Elevate Your Cleaning Routine From Laundry To Home Care With The Laundress®. Shop Now!"
- Sitelinks on Activewear ad: Kits & Gifts, Shop Our Fabric Care Sets, The Signature Detergent, Home Cleaning Products
- Sitelinks on Stain Solution ad: "Powerful Treatment For Stains," "The Stain Solution"
- 100% off shipping on orders over $50 noted in one display ad
- Rating extensions visible: 4.4 (507 reviews) on cashmere ad, 4.5 (751 reviews) on stain solution ad

**Visual approach:** Shopping ads use clean bottle-on-white product images. Display/lifestyle ads show product in-use or editorial product arrangements. Video ad thumbnails use dark editorial backgrounds with text overlays and lifestyle footage. The Indulgent Detergent Collection ad shows multiple dark/forest green bottles grouped together — distinct visual from the main clear-bottle product line.

**Gaps vs. Meta:** Google Shopping features the Signature Detergent prominently as a purchasable product (with price). Meta ads do not run the Signature Detergent as a PDP destination in any of the three captured ads — confirming the user's observation. Google leans heavily into educational intent capture ("How To Clean Cashmere," "How To Wash Wool") and product-specific shopping inventory. Meta ads focus on three different products (Delicate Wash, Mesh Bundle, Stain Solution) with brand-level copy framing. The Stain Solution appears across both channels (Ad 3 on Meta, shopping + search ad on Google), but the Signature Detergent — the highest-review, best-selling product — is only surfaced on Google.
