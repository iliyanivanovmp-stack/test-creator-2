# Meta Ads Visual Summary

## Ad 1
**Creative:** Sponsored Flo Mattress video ad featuring Bhuvan Bam and Sameer. Copy says the mattress is trusted by thousands with 95% 5-star reviews and lists 3D Air-Flo technology and 100% natural latex. The CTA is “Order now.”
**Landing Page Fold 1:** Desktop homepage hero with mattress and rolled mattress imagery, the headline “Unlock your deepest sleep, or get 100% refund.”, “Up to 40% off,” pricing from ₹9 per night, and a “Shop Mattress” CTA. A strip above the hero lists free shipping, a 100-night risk-free trial, and a 10-year warranty. No-cost EMI logos and a row of vertical video tiles appear below.
**Landing Page Fold 2:** Product cards with prices, compare-at prices, discount percentages, and “Shop Now” buttons appear above a “Go with the Flo” product-feature section. Visible feature content covers spine support and cooling technology.
**Landing Page Fold 3:** Product-detail content describes an aloe vera-infused cover, followed by a “Choose from 5 amazing options” section with mattress option cards including Ergo and Ortho.
**LP CTA:** “Shop Mattress” appears inside the hero; “Shop Now” buttons appear on the product cards. No sticky CTA bar is visible in the collected desktop folds.
**LP trust signals:** Free shipping, 100-night risk-free trial, and 10-year warranty appear directly above the hero. A “100% refund” promise appears in the hero headline. Bank logos appear beneath the hero for zero-percent EMI. The ad’s 95% 5-star review claim is not visible near the hero CTA; a vertical “Reviews” tab is fixed along the left edge.
**Message Match:** Both the ad and homepage present Flo mattresses and mattress technology. The homepage visibly includes product technology and purchase options, while the ad’s Bhuvan Bam/Sameer framing, 95% 5-star claim, and natural-latex claim are not repeated in the collected homepage hero.
