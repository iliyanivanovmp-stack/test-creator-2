# Google Ads Visual Summary

**Screenshots reviewed:** google-ads-1.png, google-ads-2.png

**Ads visible:** 3+ search text ads, 20+ shopping PLAs, 2 display/video ads, 1 local search ad

**Headline themes:**
- "Mondays Made Jewelry - Flower-Inspired Jewelry"
- "Mondays Made Jewelry - 10% Off Your First Order"
- "Whimsical Pieces for Your Soul - Hypoallergenic & Tarnish Free"
- "Mondays Made Jewelry - Versatile Reversible Jewelry"
- Shopping product headlines: individual product names (Carved Sunflower Necklace, Carved Lily Necklace, Carved Sakura Ring/Necklace, Purple Orchid Ring, Periwinkle Blossom Ring, Lilac Sunset Moissanite Reversible, Citrine Bee Necklace, Blue Cluster Flower Bracelet, Carved Hydrangea Cluster Necklace, Carved Pink Rose Necklace, Carved Cosmos Necklace, Carved Poppy Necklace, Tulip Necklace, Puffy Seashell Open Ring)

**Offers and CTAs:**
- Primary offer: 10% off first order with free shipping worldwide (repeated across 2 search ad variants)
- "Buy now, pay later available" mentioned in one search ad
- "Complimentary Gifts" listed as a sitelink alongside Mondays Maker Club and FAQ
- Free shipping mentioned in search ad body copy
- Shopping ads show product prices (not visible in all cards but some show [Price] placeholder indicating dynamic insertion)

**Visual approach:**
- Shopping PLAs: product-only images on neutral/white backgrounds, clean and consistent across the catalogue
- Display/video ads: dark green background with lifestyle close-up of flower jewelry on skin and text overlays ("show your heartfelt appreciation" on one, "nature-inspired 18k gold, rose gold & silver jewelry from ethically-sourced gemstones" on another)
- Local ad format: one ad shows a map/business card format with Website, Call, Directions CTAs and the headline "Unique Reversible Jewelry"

**Gaps vs. Meta:**
- Meta ads make no mention of the 10% off first-order offer; Google search ads lead with it
- Meta ad copy centers on craftsmanship ("carved from natural gemstones," "5x thicker plating") — Google search ads use "whimsical soul," "dainty pieces," "luxurious look in accessible prices," which is a softer positioning angle
- "Hypoallergenic & Tarnish Free" appears as a Google headline with no direct Meta equivalent
- Google display ads mention "ethically-sourced gemstones" — not referenced in any of the 3 Meta ads captured
- Reversible jewelry is featured in Google (dedicated ad + local ad) but not in Meta ads captured
- Gifting positioning appears in Google ("Complimentary Gifts" sitelink, "perfect gifts to the loved ones or yourself") and on Ad 2 LP (Malala Fund giving-back note) but not in Meta ad copy directly
