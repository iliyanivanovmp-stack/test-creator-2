### Recurring Friction (3-Star Reviews)

**1. Satiety falls short of the "5 hours" promise:**
Multiple reviewers report hunger returning within 1-3 hours, not 5.
- "nur am Abend kommt der Hunger nach 1 Stunde wieder" - Christine T., Feb 2026
- "macht es auch nicht mehr 4h satt sondern nur noch 3h" after 8 weeks - Daniel, Feb 2026 (SHEKO reply: admitted possible undereating)
- "Sättigung erfolgt leider nicht wenn man das Produkt mit Milch anrührt" - Mone, Oct 2025
- "Die Shakes selbst machen nicht satt" - Cbd, Mar 2025
- "kaum Sättigungsgefühl" - Hefra, Jan 2026
- SHEKO's own CS replies to every satiety complaint recommend: adding Abnehmkur (glucomannan), extra fiber, magerquark instead of milk. This implies the base shake alone does not reliably deliver 5-hour satiety.

**2. Taste too bland or too sweet (split audience):**
- Too bland: "alle drei Sorten so geschmacksneutral, dass man kaum erkennen kann, welche Sorte man gerade trinkt" - Nicole D., Nov 2025
- "Espresso kann man den Kaffee nur erahnen" - Mone, Oct 2025
- Too sweet: "viel zu stark gesüsst" - baltis, May 2025; "zu süß" - C. Wagner, Feb 2026
- Vegan shakes specifically criticized: "sehr dickflüssig und mehlig" (very thick and floury)
- One reviewer noted an "animalischen Nebengeschmack" (animal/meaty aftertaste) - Jürg Knobel, Feb 2026

**3. Stealth price increase (client-actionable, not a test idea):**
- Can size reduced from 450g to 378g at the same price = 16% shrinkflation
- Collagen reduced from 50 to 30 portions at same price
- Kristin Schmidt (Mar 2026): "60 Portionen weniger...Die Umstellung erfolgte ohne großes Aufsehen...hat einen sauren Beigeschmack, schon fast wie die Strategie bei Milka"
- Anonymous (3 stars): "Preiserhöhung von über 19%. Ganz schön frech!!!"
- SHEKO responded: "announced in December newsletter and Facebook community" - but many customers missed it
- Risk: Long-term customers noticing the change are the most likely to churn

**4. No results for some users:**
- "Nehme es jetzt 8 Wochen +1.5kg" - Daniel, Feb 2026 (following instructions precisely, gained weight)
- "keine Abnahme, manchmal auch zu" - Heike G., Oct 2025
- SHEKO's response consistently directs these customers to calorie calculator and lifestyle adjustments

**5. Fulfillment/shipping issues (multiple periods):**
- Recurring pattern: 2-3 week waits, missing items, no order confirmation emails
- Most prominent in July-August 2025 and January 2026 (high-demand periods)
- "seit 3 Wochen in Bearbeitung und auf Anfragen gibt es keine Reaktion" - Mars, Jul 2025
- Phone support not available; support response times criticized
- Some Italian customers received first responses in German (language system error)

**6. Limited/seasonal flavors:**
- Popular flavors (Coffee, Hazelnut) go out of stock
- "schade ist nur dass manche Geschmacksrichtungen nur für bestimmte Zeit zu kaufen sind" - Leigh Murphy, Mar 2026

## Source 6: CRO Ebook (Billion Dollar Websites - Dylan Ander)

Relevant sections pulled based on themes emerging from the data:

**On Headlines (Dream Outcome vs. Vessel):** "Headlines are supposed to tell your customers the end result of using your product... It's the subheadline's job to describe the product." The current homepage headline "Balance gewinnen oder Gewicht verlieren?" fails this. It is a question, not a result. The ads prove what the Dream Outcome looks like: "-8kg in 8 Wochen."

**On Buy Box friction:** "Make the Price Obvious. Do not bury your price, no matter how high the price is." SHEKO does this well (price is prominent). However, a gated CTA (requiring 3 flavor selections before activating) introduces friction directly in the Buy Box.

**On Social Proof at scale:** "You can use badges that state things like 'More than 10,000+ customers'... This is an incredible signal to increase trust right above the fold." SHEKO has 24,643 reviews but only shows 282 on the PDP.

**On Pre-Purchase Cart Upsells:** "Pre-purchase upsells are 'one-click buys' in your Cart and are generally great for increasing AOV... One-click upsells need to be true impulse purchases. They should be complementary products that are much cheaper compared to the main product." The Abnehmkur at €14.95 vs. the €89.92 bundle is the ideal ratio.

**Upsell characteristics required:** Discounted (min 25% off), relevant, one-click, descriptive. The Abnehmkur ticks all four.

## Cross-Source Analysis: Converging Themes

### Theme 1: CTA friction at the moment of purchase (HIGH CONFIDENCE)
Sources: Site Screenshots, CRO Community Research, Ebook
The primary CTA on the highest-traffic page (PDP, all ad traffic) appears inactive until all 3 flavor selections are completed. Users arrive primed to buy (from direct-response Meta ads), then must make 3 sequential decisions before they can even click "add to cart." CRO research documents 47%+ CR lift from reducing this exact type of friction.

### Theme 2: Social proof gap between homepage and PDP (HIGH CONFIDENCE)
Sources: Site Screenshots (homepage vs. PDP)
Homepage product cards show 24,643 reviews. PDP shows 282. Same product. Every ad-click visitor lands on the weaker social proof page. The "10,000+ purchases in 2 months" badge is present on the PDP, but the 24,643 review count is not.

### Theme 3: Cart has zero AOV optimization (HIGH CONFIDENCE)
Sources: Site Screenshots (cart), Reviews, Ebook, CRO Community Research
The Abnehmkur Berry (€14.95) solves the #1 complaint in reviews (insufficient satiety) and is the product SHEKO's own CS team recommends. It is not mentioned anywhere in the cart. A 43% revenue lift from cart cross-sell is documented in a comparable Shopify context.

### Theme 4: Homepage hero doesn't match ad message (HIGH CONFIDENCE)
Sources: Meta Ads, Google Ads, Site Screenshots, Ebook
Ads lead with specific results ("-8kg in 8 Wochen"). Homepage hero asks a hedging question and invites visitors to "discover." The Dream Outcome (weight loss results) is proven in ad copy. The homepage headline doesn't deliver it. Transformation proof (Iris, Carina, Mathias) is buried in fold 3.

### Theme 5: Message mismatch - 8-week ads landing on 4-week product (MEDIUM CONFIDENCE)
Sources: Meta Ads, Site Screenshots
Ads 1 & 2 (active since Dec 28, 2025) promise 8-week results and land on a 4-week product. SHEKO appears to have caught this (Ad 3 from Feb 2026 uses 4-week claims). Ads 1 & 2 were still active at time of analysis.

### Theme 6: Satiety credibility gap (MEDIUM CONFIDENCE - impacts retention more than acquisition)
Sources: Reviews, Site Screenshots (PDP claims "5 Stunden satt")
Multiple 3-star reviews cite satiety falling short. SHEKO's own CS responses consistently recommend the Abnehmkur as the satiety fix. This is more of a product positioning and retention issue, but the cart upsell opportunity directly addresses it.
