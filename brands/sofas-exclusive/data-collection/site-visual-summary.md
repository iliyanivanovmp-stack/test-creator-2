Announcement bar: "Free & Fast Shipping Within Continental US. See More." Three trust badges directly below nav: "30-Days Free Returns", "White Glove Delivery", "Secure Payment." Full-width hero image of a curved cream/white sofa in a minimal earthy interior. Centered white headline: "Lounge in Luxury." Subheadline: "Experience furniture crafted to blend modern aesthetics." Two side-by-side white CTA buttons: "VIEW MORE" and "SHOP NOW."

**Fold 2:** Dark background. "Customers are saying" heading. Rating: 4.91 stars (11 reviews), Verified badge. Single rotating testimonial card: "I can't thank Sofas Exclusive enough for their exceptional service and easy to navigate..." — Stephan W., Aphrodite Loveseat. Carousel navigation dots visible. No product imagery in this section.

**Fold 3:** "FEATURED BRANDS" label on dark background. Four partner/brand logos displayed: Dienne, S/E (Sofas Exclusive monogram), mater, SIMPLI HOME. "New Arrivals" heading below. Four-column product grid beginning to appear — dark background cards with chair and sofa images. Content cut off at fold edge.

**Layout notes:** Entire homepage uses a dark charcoal/near-black background. Typography is light-colored on dark. Trust badges sit between nav and hero — prominent placement. Review section appears above the fold break, using a single testimonial carousel with a low review count (11) shown explicitly. No product prices visible on homepage. CTA buttons are minimal white rectangles with no visual hierarchy differentiation between "VIEW MORE" and "SHOP NOW."

Single line item: THE VOLOS Chair, product thumbnail, "Remove" link, quantity stepper (− 1 +), price USD 278.00.
