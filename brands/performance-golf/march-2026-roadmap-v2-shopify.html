9 slots · 3 months · Profitability layer, trust, PDP optimization + product discovery
Insights

Performance Golf is moving from Checkout Champ to Shopify. The old system's profitability depended on post-purchase video upsells, a mechanism that doesn't exist on the new platform yet. Rebuilding it is the top priority: without it, the economics don't work.

The second priority is trust. Across 35+ recent Trustpilot reviews, customers cite unwanted subscription charges and hidden fees. Yet PG holds the strongest guarantee in the competitive set (365 days vs. the industry average of 60) and has 800K+ clubs sold, 1M+ subscribers, and 3,700+ reviews at 4.8 stars. These signals are invisible at the point of purchase.

Shopify is launching ChatGPT as an agentic storefront channel. Buyers will find and purchase products directly inside ChatGPT. What the AI pulls is shaped by your product content: gallery images, descriptions, and pricing structure. Three high-impact PDP tests to make sure Performance Golf shows up right.
