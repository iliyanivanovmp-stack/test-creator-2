# R for Rabbit Roadmap Seed

**Store:** https://rforrabbit.com/
**AOV:** unknown
**Monthly sessions:** unknown
**Data sources:** manifest, Reviews & UGC, Meta ads visual summary, Google ads visual summary, site visual summary, PageSpeed JSON, live store fetch, competitor research

## Key Insights

Review data covers 76 reviews from 2025-10-15 to 2026-02-10: 57 five-star, 4 four-star, 1 two-star, and 14 one-star. Customers love comfort, softness, safety, and breadth; quotes include "The softest diapers ever witnessed", "diapers are very gentle and rash free", and "comfortable and last long."

The highest-risk contradiction is trust: ads/site copy sell safety and quality, while one-star reviews cite delayed delivery, no updates, wrong items, tampered products, refund delays, support disconnects, email non-response, and one alleged order-data leak. Promo clarity is fragmented across `NEW12`, `TRYSAMPLE`, `DPR15`, `DAYONE`, `FFRIST`, app savings, cashback, threshold savings, and subscription savings.

PageSpeed data from 2026-06-09 shows homepage Performance 27, LCP 26.5s, TBT 6,030ms; Feather Diapers PDP Performance 29, LCP 20.0s, TBT 7,580ms. Competitor pressure is price/review-count driven: Babyhug has high rating counts and per-diaper pricing; LuvLap stroller pricing overlaps R for Rabbit.

## Top Test Opportunities

### 1. Fix Broken Product Card Error Text on Homepage and Collection
**What's broken:** On homepage carousels and the `All` collection grid, each product card shows image, title, rating, price, then a red liquid/template error above the outlined `Add to Cart` button. This sits in the price/CTA zone, making the buying path look broken.
**Evidence:** Site visual summary, homepage screenshots, collection screenshots.
**Key data:** Error text appears across `Most Loved Products`, `Just Launched`, and repeated Feather Diapers collection cards.
**Est. lift:** 2.0% CR lift x unknown sessions/mo x unknown AOV = unknown.

### 2. Unify First-Order Offer Messaging From Ad to PDP to Cart
**What's broken:** Ad 1 promotes `NEW12`, Ad 2 `TRYSAMPLE`, Ad 3 `DPR15`, and Google also shows `FFRIST`, while header/cart emphasize `DAYONE`, app savings, cashback, and threshold savings. The active deal changes between ad, PDP, and cart.
**Evidence:** Meta ads summary, Google ads summary, PDP screenshots, cart screenshot.
**Key data:** Cart says `Use Coupon: DAYONE`; PDP cards show `DPR15` and `DAYONE`.
**Est. lift:** 1.5% CR lift x unknown sessions/mo x unknown AOV = unknown.
