# Google Ads Visual Summary

**Screenshots reviewed:** google-ads-1.png, google-ads-2.png, google-ads-3.png  
**Ads visible:** Multiple search-style text ads, display/video creatives, shopping-style product tiles, sitelink-heavy ad cards, and several unavailable ad placeholders. Visible ads include product categories such as strollers, tricycles, high chairs, feeding bottles, baby carriers, carry cots, car seats, diaper bags, scooters, bicycles, and diapers.  
**Headline themes:** Headlines mention sale events, `R for Rabbit Official Store`, safe baby products, safe feeding chairs, high chairs, baby strollers, baby cycles, diaper bags, carry cots, scooters, and category-specific product benefits. Several headlines use promotional language such as `Sale Is Live`, `Biggest Sale Ever`, `Up To 50% off`, `Premium`, `Safe`, `Stylish`, and `Designed with Parents in Mind`.  
**Offers and CTAs:** Visible offers include sale messaging, cash on delivery/free shipping claims in snippets, `Up to 50% off Big Festive Sale`, `Save UpTo Rs.1000`, `15% off on First Diaper - Code DPR15`, `Code FFRIST`, and app or event sale references.  
**Visual approach:** Display/video creatives show product-led imagery with children using scooters, ride-on toys, high chairs, feeding products, diapers, and stroller/car seat products. Some image ads use promotional frames, discount badges, product pack shots, and short-video style thumbnails.  
**Gaps vs. Meta:** Meta screenshots collected focus on stroller and Feather Diaper offers. Google Ads screenshots show those themes plus a broader category spread across feeding, car seats, cycles, scooters, carriers, and official store/category pages. The diaper code `DPR15` appears in both Meta Ad 3 and Google Ads; Google Ads also shows several other promo codes and sale themes not visible in the Meta ad creatives.
