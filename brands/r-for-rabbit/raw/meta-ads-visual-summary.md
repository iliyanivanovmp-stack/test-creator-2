# Meta Ads Visual Summary

## Ad 1
**Creative:** Facebook/Meta ad for R for Rabbit showing a black Rock N Roll 2 In 1 Baby Stroller Cum Rocker on a simple studio-style product platform. The ad copy mentions parenting starts with essentials, saving big on premium newborn gear, up to 45% off, extra 12% off with code `NEW12`, and a `Shop Now` CTA.  
**Landing Page Fold 1:** Product page for `Rock N Roll 2 In 1 Baby Stroller Cum Rocker, Light Weight, Compact Travel Friendly, One Hand Fold - Black`. The fold shows the top coupon strip, header navigation, large stroller product image, star rating, price `₹ 6,998`, app savings banner, color swatches, `Add To Cart`, `Add Free Customization`, pincode checker, offer cards, trust icons, and a floating `Watch & Buy` video widget.  
**Landing Page Fold 2:** Frequently bought together module with stroller and rocker product cards, combined total price, `Add To Cart`, star ratings, and a row of large media/UGC-style tiles showing stroller usage and product views.  
**Landing Page Fold 3:** Product detail area with a large embedded YouTube video titled around the Rock N Roll stroller, descriptive product text, and a below-the-fold product image section beginning with `ROCK N ROLL`.  
**Message Match:** The ad creative promotes the Rock N Roll stroller cum rocker, and the landing page displays the same product, price, stroller imagery, add-to-cart path, and supporting stroller media. The ad mentions up to 45% off and code `NEW12`; the visible landing page offer cards show other offers including `DAYONE`, app savings, and free customization.
