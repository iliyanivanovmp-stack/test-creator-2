**Fold 3:** Product card carousel continues into `Just Launched`, showing baby walker and diaper bag products, ratings, prices, red liquid error text, and `Add to Cart` buttons. `Shop by Age` appears below with circular age options: `0-6 Months`, `6-12 Months`, `1-2 Years`, `2-4 Years`, `4-6 Years`, and `6+ Years`.  
**Layout notes:** Homepage uses a full-width hero carousel, centered content width for product rows, green accent color for navigation/controls, floating `My Cashback` and WhatsApp widgets, and repeated carousel arrows. Product cards include image, title, rating, price, error text, and outlined add-to-cart controls.

**Layout notes:** Collection page uses a sticky-feeling header, persistent left filters, four-column desktop grid, and repeated product card structure. The visible collection screenshots show many repeated diaper variants rather than mixed category products.
