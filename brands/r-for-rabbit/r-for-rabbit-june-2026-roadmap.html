Cross-source themes

Biggest Killers of Conversion Rate

  • Trust is the highest-leverage theme. Reviews show strong product love, but negative reviews concentrate around delivery, refunds, support, tampered or wrong products, and one data-security fear.
  • Offer clarity is fragmented across Meta, Google, homepage, PDP, and cart surfaces. Shoppers can see NEW12, TRYSAMPLE, DPR15, DAYONE, FFRIST, app savings, cashback, threshold savings, and subscription savings.
  • Product-card and PDP paths have avoidable friction. The audit documents red liquid error text on product cards, repeated diaper variants in collection, a heavy first-fold PDP decision load, and slow mobile lab performance.

Meta Ads and Landing Pages

  • Ad 1 promotes Rock N Roll with up to 45% off and NEW12, while the PDP shows other offers including DAYONE, app savings, and free customization.
  • Ad 2 promotes Feather Diaper Sample at ₹3 with TRYSAMPLE, but the PDP also shows DPR15 and DAYONE.
  • Ad 3 promotes Feather Diapers with DPR15, while the PDP introduces subscription, cashback, app savings, and bundle modules before description content.

Google Ads

  • Google Ads span strollers, tricycles, high chairs, feeding bottles, baby carriers, carry cots, car seats, diaper bags, scooters, bicycles, and diapers.
  • Promo language includes DPR15, FFRIST, app and event sale references, cash on delivery or free shipping snippets, and savings up to ₹1000.
  • DPR15 appears in both Meta and Google Ads, but the site header and cart emphasize DAYONE.

Reviews and UGC

  • The review dataset covers 76 dated reviews from 2025-10-15 through 2026-02-10: 57 five-star, 4 four-star, 1 two-star, and 14 one-star reviews.
  • Positive reviews repeatedly praise comfort and softness, especially for Feather Diapers, with quotes such as "The softest diapers ever witnessed" and "comfortable and last long."
  • Negative reviews center on delayed delivery, no delivery updates, wrong items, delayed refunds, agents disconnecting, email non-response, IVR dead ends, tampered or open products, and one alleged order-data leak.

PageSpeed Insights

  • Homepage mobile lab performance was 27, with LCP 26.5s, FCP 3.8s, Speed Index 12.6s, TBT 6,030ms, TTI 56.2s, CLS 0.068, and 1,718 KiB of unused JavaScript savings.
  • Feather Diapers PDP mobile lab performance was 29, with LCP 20.0s, FCP 3.4s, Speed Index 18.7s, TBT 7,580ms, TTI 75.1s, CLS 0.007, and 1,632 KiB of unused JavaScript savings.
  • The audit identifies waiting and main-thread blockage as the highest conversion risk, not layout shift.

Current Site Screenshots

  • The homepage first fold includes DAYONE, navigation, search, app download, account, order, cart, a hero carousel, and horizontal savings tiles.
  • Homepage and collection product cards include image, title, rating, price, red liquid error text, and Add to Cart controls.
  • The Feather Diapers PDP first fold shows the subscription banner, gallery, title, 4.7 | 939, SKU, ₹394, app savings, cashback, style selector, size guide, size and pack dropdowns, subscription and one-time selectors, and Add To Cart.
  • The cart drawer shows Use Coupon: DAYONE, Add ₹1106 more to save ₹100, savings milestones from ₹100 to ₹800 off, app savings, a Feather Diapers line item, coupon field, pincode field, estimated total, You are saving ₹0, and Check Out.

Competitor Research

  • FirstCry/Babyhug, Mee Mee, LuvLap, Mothercare, and Chicco were identified as relevant competitors.
  • R for Rabbit live site claims 500+ products, 5M+ happy parents, and 4.5+ rating across platforms.
  • Competitor evidence includes LuvLap stroller prices from ₹3,999 to ₹6,999, Babyhug diaper pants prices from about ₹530 to ₹1,286, and Mee Mee Ultra Sleek Portable Baby High Chair at Rs. 8,499.

Missing Data
