Executive Summary

We analyzed Shopify analytics, customer surveys, site search, reviews, ads, your content calendar, and Intelligems data to identify the highest-leverage tests for May. The concepts below cover product discovery, the checkout, the homepage, Hello Summer Sale (the biggest May revenue moment), and the PDP.

May Testing Concepts
01
Concept 1 A/B Test

Product Discovery Quiz

Standalone Quiz Page

Over 78,000 site searches in 30 days are dominated by print names: Cat, Bagheera, Mahjong, Bagel, Caviar. New visitors have no efficient way to navigate a high-SKU catalog. The brand already maps prints to lifestyle personas in email marketing. A quiz translates that into a shoppable on-site experience that reduces decision paralysis.

* Concept illustration only. Final design will differ.

02
Concept 2 A/B Test

Checkout Upsells

Checkout

Testing checkout upsells using Intelligems' new in-checkout feature. The opportunity is structural: average units per order is 1.56 (most shoppers buy a single item). We'll research order data to identify frequently-bought-together combinations and evaluate the best items to test as one-click upsells in the checkout flow.

* Concept illustration only. Final design will differ.

03
Concept 3 A/B Test

Homepage 2nd Fold

Homepage

Follow-up to the homepage test currently running. The 2nd fold today routes shoppers into broad category tiles, but site searches are dominated by print names (Cat, Bagheera, Mahjong, Bagel), and the brand is highly seasonal with frequent launches, sales, and themed collections that the 2nd fold could promote far more effectively than the current static grid. We'll use heatmap data to evaluate how users interact with the current tiles and the categories from the mega menu, then optimize the 2nd fold of the highest-traffic page.

* Concept illustration only. Final design will differ.

04
Concept 4 A/B Test

Hello Summer Sale Activation

Scope TBD

HSS is the May revenue moment. We'll review past sale tests from the brand and the broader portfolio, then scope the highest-leverage test once we know how the sale will be promoted (dedicated collection page, homepage hero, sitewide announcement bar, or a combination).

* Concept illustration only. Final design will differ.

05
Concept 5 A/B Test

PDP Optimization Stack

Product Page

Follow-up to the PDP test currently running. Customer data points to a stack of optimization opportunities: fabric education (reviews calling out description mismatch), cross-silhouette print discovery (reviews asking for the same print in other silhouettes), colorway visibility (multi-colorway prints show only one swatch today), sizing selection UI, and image gallery UI. The next PDP test will combine the highest-leverage ideas.

* Concept illustration only. Final design will differ.
